Dosa & Sambar

Ingredients


    For Dosa

  • Urad Dal – 1/3rd Cup
  • Rice – 1Cup
  • Refined Oil – 8-10 Tbsp
  • Garam Masala- 2 tsp
  • Salt- ¼ tea spoon or as per taste

    • For Sambar

  • Toor Daal – 1 cup
  • Drumstick – 1 diced
  • Brinjal – 1 diced
  • Pumpkin – 1cup diced
  • Tomato – 1 diced
  • Tamarind paste 20gms (lemon size ball)
  • Curry Leaves – 7-10
  • Garlic – 6-7 buds chopped/ crushed
  • Ginger – 1finely chopped
  • Green Chilli- 2 chopped
  • Mustard Seeds- ½ Tea spoon
  • Cumin Seeds- ½ Tea spoon
  • Red Chilli Powder – ½ Tea spoon
  • Sambar Masala – 1 Table spoon
  • Turmeric Powder – ½ Tea spoon
  • Salt as per taste

Procedure


    Preparation of Dosa

  1. Wash and soak rice for 5 - 6 hrs separately.
  2. Take tur dal, chana dal, methi seeds and soak them.
  3. Wash and clean urad dal and soak it overnight in a separate bowl.
  4. Grind rice and mixture of dal separately with minimal required water else the mixture will not be as smooth as required for batter.
  5. After grinding separately mix them properly in a larger mixing bowl.
  6. Mix water to make the batter to required consistency.
  7. Consistency should neither be thick nor be very thin as it will be difficult for spreading on dosa pan.
  8. Add salt as per taste and mix thoroughly.
  9. Now cover it with lid and leave overnight for fermentation. After fermentation the batter will be lighter and fluffy.
  10. Put a flat pan on heat and keep it on low flame.
  11. Put 1 teaspoon oil and one ladle batter on it and then spread it on evenly.
  12. If pan is very hot it will be difficult to spread batter evenly.
  13. Hence Keep a bowl of water along with and sprinkle few drops on pan and wipe it off with clean cloth to avoid excessive heating of dosa pan.
  14. Sprinkle some garam masala & few drops of butter on it.
  15. Once cooked the sides will automatically leave the pan. Then take it out on a plate.

    16. Preparation of Sambar

  16. Pressure cook tur dal until cooked (3-4 whistle). You may add vegetables like brinjal, pumpkin, Drumsticks, tomato along with dal.
  17. Add only salt and turmeric powder to cook dal. You can also use leftover dal to make sambar
  18. In the meanwhile make tamarind paste by soaking it with water and squeeze the pulp into water and remove seeds if it has.
  19. Heat oil in a pan and Add one tsp hing or asafoetida.
  20. Add one tsp mustard seeds and cumin seeds and let it splutter.
  21. Add curry leaves, crushed garlic and ginger and Then add sliced onions and green chillies.
  22. Saute for 2 mins.
  23. Add chopped tomatoes and saute till it's cooked and mashy.
  24. Next add ½ tsp red chilli powder, 2 - 3 tbsp sambar masala.
  25. Then add tamarind paste and diced tomato into the pan and let it simmer for 1 – 2 min.
  26. Add cooked dal to it and a little water if it's thicker consistency.
  27. Bring it to a boil for 3-4 mins. Add salt as per taste. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  28. Serve hot dosa with sambar.
