Korean scallion Pancakes

Ingredients

  • flour-2 cups
  • Eggs beaten-2 large
  • kosher salt-1 teaspoon
  • scallions, green and white parts; halved lengthwise and cut into 2- to 3-inch lengths-1 bunch
  • water-1 1/2 cups
  • vegetable oil, for frying-3 tablespoons
  • soy sauce

Procedure

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, eggs, salt, scallions, and water, and let sit for about 10 minutes.
  3. Check the consistency before cooking.
  4. The batter should be a little bit runnier than American pancake batter which allows the pa jun to cook quickly and evenly.
  5. Add more water if needed.
  6. Heat a sauté pan over medium heat and coat it with a thin layer of oil.
  7. Pour batter to fill the pan in a thin layer (about 1/3 of your batter should fill a regular sauté pan, but the amount will depend on the size of your pan).
  8. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes until set and golden brown on the bottom.
  9. Flip the pancake over with the help of a spatula or plate (or flip it in the air if you are good at that).
  10. Finish by cooking 1 to 2 more minutes, adding more oil if necessary.
  11. Serve with soy sauce or a spicy dipping sauce. Enjoy.
