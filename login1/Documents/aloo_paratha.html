Aloo paratha

Ingredients

  • whole wheat flour-250 gm
  • Pinch of salt
  • water-1 cup
  • Oil-50 ml
  • potatoes-500 gm
  • coriander leaves-20 gm
  • ginger garlic paste-20 gm
  • Onions-75 gm
  • green chillies-5 gm
  • turmeric-5 gm
  • chilli powder-5 gm
  • lime juice-10 ml

Procedure

  1. Make a soft dough with the specified ingredients.
  2. Divide the dough into balls, as per the required size.
  3. Chop the onions, green chillies and the coriander leaves.
  4. Then boil the potatoes; remove the skin and mash.
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well.
  6. Take each portion of the dough, flatten it on palm.
  7. Stuff the filling in the dough and roll into balls.
  8. With a rolling pin, flatten the balls into 1/2" thick round parathas.
  9. Grease a pan with a little oil and heat on a medium flame.
  10. Cook the parathas until both sides are golden and cooked through.
  11. Serve hot
