Banana bread frenchtoast

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 4
  • low-fat milk-3/4 cup
  • vanilla extract-1 teaspoon
  • ground cinnamon-Â¼ teaspoon
  • banana bread, about 1 inch thick-8 slices
  • butter, divided-2 tablespoons
  • Toasted chopped walnuts, sliced banana and pure maple syrup for topping

Procedure

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. After cutting the banana bread, beat together the eggs, milk, salt, sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  3. Heat a griddle or a large, heavy-bottomed skillet on high heat.
  4. Spray with nonstick cooking spray or add a tablespoon of butter.
  5. Dip a few pieces of the banana bread into the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  6. Place the banana bread facing down on the hot griddle, turn the heat to medium-low (this helps prevent the French toast from burning or caramelizing too quickly).
  7. Cook for about 2 minutes per side and Remove and keep warm in a 250 F oven.
  8. Repeat the cooking with the remaining pieces of banana bread.
  9. Serve the warm banana bread with a sprinkling of confectioners' sugar, a drizzle of maple syrup, and walnuts if desired.
